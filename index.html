Welcome to the Vital Signs Dashboard

Exploratory laparoscopy is a minimally invasive surgical procedure that uses a small camera and specialized instruments inserted through tiny incisions to examine internal organs. Our project focuses on analyzing Exploratory Laparoscopy procedures using data sourced from VitalDB, a comprehensive dataset of 6,388 surgical patients' clinical information. This project analyzes data to uncover critical insights about vital signs during surgery. By comparing trends in survivors versus non‑survivors, our visualization aims to identify key indicators of patient outcomes. Watch our video to learn more!

By: Sarah He, Hao Zhang, Kevin Zhang
